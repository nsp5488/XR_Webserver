Uploading a Presentation from Google Slides

  1. Download your slides from google presentations as a PDF

    1. File < Download < PDF

  2. Upload your slides to the slide upload website
    1. Click 'Choose File'
    2. Select the slides that you just downloaded
    3. Click 'Submit'
    4. Wait a few seconds
    5. You should get moved to a screen that looks like this, with your 5 digit code on it
  3. Access the slides in the app
    1. Launch the app in the VR headset
    2. You will be greeted with a main menu
    3. Click 'Load Presentation'
    4. Type in the code that you received from the website by pointing at the numpad on screen and pressing the trigger button on the right VR controller
    5. Click 'Start'
    6. Wait a few seconds
    7. You should be brought into the classroom scene where you can give the presentation
  4. Giving the presentation
    1. Once you are in the classroom scene your slides should be on both the monitor in front of you and on the projector behind you
    2. Press the 'A' button on the right controller to advance a slide
    3. Press the 'B' button on the right controller to go back a slide
    4. There is a timer on the desk next to the laptop with a 'Start' and a 'Pause' button, point at either button and press the trigger on the right controller to interact with them.
    5. On the other side of the desk, there are two buttons, one which lets you restart the presentation you are giving, and one that brings you back to the main menu. You also interact with these by pointing at them and pressing trigger on the right controller
  5. Any additional questions should be answerable by watching this video walkthrough
